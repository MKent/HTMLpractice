A Bit About Us!

Two Many Cooks is a family style restaurant founded and operated by brother and sister combo Jared and Sarah Kent. Jared attended Johnson and Wales University in Charlotte, North Carolina, where he mastered the culinary arts. After attaining his bachelors, he came home to Cleveland, Ohio, and opened shop with his sister. They special in hometown specials and overall comfort food, with a bit of twist.
